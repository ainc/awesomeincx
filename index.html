The Accelerator

Awesome Inc X

"Awesome Inc accelerated my startup. The program helped my company to secure funding, add advisors, and launch my product."

The Awesome Inc Experience is a 3 month program full of intense focus on your startup. It includes investment money for your company, free office space, advice from mentors, and an environment designed to maximize the success of your startup.

Our approach

1. Apply

Two tips:
Awesome Inc Core Value #4 - Be You.
Awesome Inc Rule #17 - Bring the Fun.

2. Get Accepted

This is kind of like dating. Our team checks out your team. If it’s a good fit, you’ll hear back from us by May 1st.

3. Do Work

How do you pack 1 year of progress into 3 months? Work smarter.

4. Start Something Awesome

Ideas are great, but we’re about action...and traction. You’re starting a movement. How big can it grow?

The Perks

Funding

$15,000 to $20,000 in seed investment

You get 15-20K for your company.

Talent

Value: $10,000

You get access to designers and developers who will work for your startup for free.

Space

Value: $5,000

You get free office space for the duration of the program in our sweet location in downtown Lexington.
